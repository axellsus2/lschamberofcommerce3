Business Onboarding Guide

Follow these steps to integrate your company into our trade ecosystem:

Step 1: Digital ID Registration

Apply for a Global Trade ID (GTID) through our secure portal using your tax credentials.

Step 2: Document Verification

Upload your Certificate of Incorporation and authorized signatory list for AML compliance.

Step 3: API Connection

Connect your ERP system to our customs clearing gateway to automate declarations.
